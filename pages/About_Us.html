About Us

Meet Team Wheelders & The Engineering Design Process in ENGI 120

Team Wheelders

Team Wheelders is an engineering design team dedicated to building the ideal set of wheels to represent Rice University for the University Rover Competition. Our rover will traverse rough terrains like gravel and sand in a race with other rovers. We are a diverse team with members from various colleges and academic backgrounds, united by our passion for engineering and innovation.
