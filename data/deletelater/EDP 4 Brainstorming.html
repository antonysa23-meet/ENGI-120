1.1In one paragraph, summarize Features and Functions. Be descriptive in how you describe each one and the interaction/connection between them.
22
Our rover wheel concept combines structural flexbility along with durability, using a core that could either be hollow for lightness or solid for added strength. It could include an outer layer made of rubber for traction or aluminum for toughness. Grousers would be added to the surface to increase grip on slope terrain, and the shape would be round to ensure stable rolling. Since the wheel cannot be pressurized, it can't pop or crack, but must remain lightweight, yet strong enough to support the rover's weight.ContributorDateHours

In a paragraph, describe your brainstorming process and results, be quantitative and specific in your description. (i.e. goal for each round, how many rounds, how much time in each round, how many total ideas from each round, how ideas were sorted in each round, name of the groupings/categories (specify if a grouping is for complete or componet/partial ideas), number of unique ideas in each, how were overlapping or repeated ideas handled?)
62
Throughout the entire brainstorming process, there are a total of two rounds. For the first round, we spent 20 minutes generating ideas about the rover wheels that came to mind. After the end of the first round, we had a total of 88 ideas. All the ideas are sorted into 5 main parts: wheel's feature design, alternative idea to a wheel, informational analysis of the wheel, ways the wheel can move, and different materials we can use within the wheel. The wheel feature design category contains complete ideas, and all the other categories contain partial ideas. We sorted duplicated ideas into one separate part. In the second round, we focused more on methods of wheel production and materials. We spent 10 minutes brainstorming and generated 20 new ideas. The ideas were sorted into materials and manufacturing methods. Overall, we have a sufficient diversity of wheel designs in two brainstorming rounds, and the unique ideas contributed to our selection of design, ways to manufacture, and types of materials.ContributorDateHours
